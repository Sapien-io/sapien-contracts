## 9. Audit Trail Requirements

### Documentation Standards
1. **Original Documents**: Keep all original invoices and receipts
2. **Payment Proof**: Bank statements or cancelled checks for all payments
3. **Project Linkage**: Clear connection between expenses and SR&ED projects
4. **Approval Chain**: Documented approval process for all expenditures
5. **Allocation Rationale**: Justification for SR&ED percentage allocations

### Record Retention
- **Minimum Period**: 6 years from end of tax year
- **Format**: Original documents preferred, digital copies acceptable
- **Organization**: By project and expense category
- **Access**: Readily available for CRA review
